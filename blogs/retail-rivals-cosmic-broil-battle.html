Have you ever imagined what it would be like to compete  trying to uncover mysterious happenings in a mall filled with surprises? Well, that's exactly what Retail Rivals: Cosmic Broil encapsulates, is a unique mall experience where anything can happen, similar to the movie Night at the Museum. The story of Retail Rivals: Cosmic Broil takes place in a mall. As the customers unravel the mysteries, they unveil a cosmical secret that sets the game's tone and opens up new levels of play. Unlike other shopping events where a shopper only comes for one thing, Cosmic Broil's mystery keeps them focused and entertained throughout the mall's various stores and objectives.

Cosmic Broil is an excellent event for those who love a bit of drama and mystery. It's like being in a fairytale world with incredible surprises on every corner. Just like the movie Night at the Museum, Retail Rivals: Cosmic Broil takes you on an adventure of a lifetime.
